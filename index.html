I am a passionate and curious individual with a strong interest in technology, creativity, and continuous learning. With a background in computer science and hands-on experience in web development and software engineering, I enjoy turning ideas into functional and visually appealing digital solutions.

Beyond technical skills, I value communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. I believe that innovation comes from asking the right questions, embracing challenges, and staying updated with evolving trends in the tech world.

Outside of coding, I enjoy learning about design, reading about emerging technologies, and occasionally engaging in creative hobbies like photography and content writing. I’m driven by the desire to make a positive impact through my work—whether it's building user-friendly applications, optimizing processes, or contributing to open-source projects.

I’m constantly seeking new opportunities to grow, collaborate, and contribute meaningfully to the tech community. My ultimate goal is to blend creativity with technology to build solutions that are not only functional but also impactful.
